# Yield Mechanics

## How BUCK Generates Value

BUCK's price appreciation comes from **external dividends** generated by a diversified hard asset treasury. The primary yield engine is STRC, Strategy's Bitcoin-collateralized preferred stock, supplemented by holdings in Bitcoin, gold, and U.S. treasuries. This is fundamentally different from competitors who generate yield through trading strategies or token emissions.

{% hint style="success" %}
**Real Yield from Real Assets**

Buck's hard asset treasury generates \~10% annual yield via contractual dividends. This yield flows directly to BUCK's exchange rate, increasing its price automatically.
{% endhint %}

## Primary Yield Source: STRC Dividends

### What is STRC?

| Property | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Issuer** | Strategy (NASDAQ: MSTR) |
| **Type** | Series A Perpetual Preferred Stock |
| **Dividend Rate** | 10% annual (paid quarterly) |
| **Backing** | Strategy's Bitcoin treasury (~$50B+) |
| **Cash Reserves** | $2.25B |
| **Dividend Coverage** | 77.4 years at current rates |

### Why STRC Dividends?

| Yield Type | Source | Can Go Negative? | Dependency |
|------------|--------|------------------|------------|
| **STRC Dividends** | Corporate preferred equity | No | Strategy solvency |
| Funding Rates | Perpetual futures | Yes | Market sentiment |
| Staking Yield | Proof of stake | No | Network activity |
| Lending Yield | Borrower interest | No | Credit demand |
| T-Bill Yield | Government bonds | No | Fed rates |

STRC dividends are:

* **Contractually obligated** — Preferred equity has legal claim
* **Paid regardless of market** — Not dependent on crypto conditions
* **Long-term sustainable** — 77+ years of coverage

## Exchange Rate Calculation

### Formula

```
BUCK Exchange Rate = Total Treasury Value / Total BUCK Supply
```

### Example

```
Treasury Holdings:
- STRC Value: $8,000,000
- USDC Reserve: $2,000,000
- Total: $10,000,000

BUCK Supply: 9,500,000 BUCK

Exchange Rate = $10,000,000 / 9,500,000 = $1.0526 per BUCK
```

